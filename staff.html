To ensure quality personnel, we maintain a stringent credentialing process that includes:

  • Criminal background check
  • DMV driving record check
  • Physical examination with TB test
  • CPR and First Aid certificate
  • Daily supervision of staff
  • Make sure that the caregiver matches the need of the client
  • Work history verification
